Bennett Designs

Hello, and welcome to my site! I'm Patrick Bennett, a Multimedia Graphic Design student at Pikes Peak State College. On these pages I hope to earn your business by showcasing my upcoming artistry. I'll treat your request seriously and dedicate myself to bringing your business needs to visual life.
I have studied design for nearly two years and am honing my skills in a variety of mediums:
  • Vector graphics in Adobe Illustrator
  • Photo restoration and photomontage in Adobe Photoshop
  • Print layout in Adobe InDesign
  • Web layout in Adobe Dreamweaver
